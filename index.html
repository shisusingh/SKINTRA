Skintra Lifesciences Private Limited is a Private incorporated on 26 July 2017. It is classified as Non-govt company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Delhi

Our Vision

To continually set new benchmarks in global healthcare with the power of human expertise, innovative research and advanced technology.

Our Mission

Contributing to better healthcare through innovation. Maximizing value for our stakeholders and customers.

Our Values

Establishing Leadership Through Progressive Efforts Maintaining the Highest Global Standards to our global network.

About Us

Revolutionizing trends in Pharmaceutical & Derma Cosmeceuticals Formulations.

Skintra is a leading, vertically integrated global pharmaceutical formulation development, manufacturing, and marketing company. Skintra is committed to challenging the unmet medical and societal needs through a comprehensive pharmaceutical value chain spanning across the world.

High–Performance

Skintra products are designed to solve skin care disorders and are conjunctive therapies to dermatological treatments. Clinically tested and veriﬁed.

High Standards

Skintra has its own R&D, Innovation Department & Quality Department, charged with maintaining exceptional standards which exceed the dermocosmetic practices.

Safety

Skintra formulations are developed for safety of use and high skin venerability, and are produced according to strict pharmaceutical industry regulations.
